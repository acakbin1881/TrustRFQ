THE PROBLEM

On-chain liquidity can't move institutional size

Stellar's deepest XLM/USDC liquidity sits in the single-digit millions. The constant-product model (x · y = k) turns punitive at scale — every block trade pays a structural slippage tax.

18%+

The same $1M clears off-chain on Coinbase or Binance OTC at under 10 bps — roughly $1,000. 100× cheaper off-chain
THE SOLUTION

RFQ execution, settled on-chain

An RFQ and intent-based layer decouples price discovery from AMM pools. Takers source competitive quotes off-book and settle them as deterministic atomic swaps — bringing institutional execution on-chain for the first time on Stellar.

0

Zero slippage

Quotes are firm and priced as a fixed amount-in for amount-out. What the maker signs is exactly what settles — no curve, no impact.

⌧

No intent leak

Block size never hits a lit orderbook. Makers compete privately, so bots can't front-run and liquidity can't flee before the trade clears.

⇄

Atomic settlement

A Soroban smart contract swaps both legs in a single transaction. Either both sides move or neither does — no counterparty risk.

HOW IT WORKS

From request to atomic swap in three steps

STEP 01

Taker opens an RFQ

An institution requests a quote for an XLM/USDC block — side and size — broadcast to the maker network.

STEP 02

Makers respond

Multiple market makers view open RFQs and reply with signed, time-limited offers competing on price.

STEP 03

Best offer settles

The taker selects the best quote and settles it as a zero-slippage atomic swap via a Soroban contract.
