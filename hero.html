How it works

From agreement to atomic swap in three steps

STEP 01

Agree & sign

You agree terms with your counterparty and create a directed order — amounts, tokens, expiry, their address. Your wallet signs the exact payload.

STEP 02

Counter-sign

The order appears live on their desk. They accept, and each wallet signs a Soroban authorization binding every argument of the fill — amounts included.

STEP 03

Atomic fill

Anyone can submit the fill carrying both signatures. Both legs move in one transaction — or nothing moves at all.

Security model

The trust lives in the signatures

No accounts, no custodian, no admin switches. Every safeguard is cryptographic — enforced by the Stellar network, not by a middleman.

Wallet = identity

No sign-up, no email, no password. Your Stellar wallet is your identity, and every action you take is a wallet signature.

Tamper-proof terms

Both signatures bind every argument of the on-chain fill. Change a single stroop and both signatures die — the transaction reverts.

Replay-guarded

Each order settles at most once: a per-order guard, per-signature nonces, and a hard expiration — all enforced on-chain.

Non-custodial

The contract never holds funds. Both legs move wallet-to-wallet inside the fill, atomically.
